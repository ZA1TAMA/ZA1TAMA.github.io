Sun Wukong

A statue sculpt of the Great Sage Equal of Heaven, from 16th century chinese epic Journey to The West I could not decide if I wanted to create a living character or a statue, which is why even though this is made of stone, it still has hair. I learned a lot about geometry nodes and Blender's hair physics during this project, as well as sculptin a primate's head and features.
